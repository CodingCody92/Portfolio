About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate web developer from Dallas, Texas, with a track record of success in driving engagement and innovation. I graduated from the University of Louisiana at Monroe in 2014 with a degree in business and a minor in marketing. While initially rooted in the business world, my passion for web development sparked a pivot from business to technology. This journey reflects my commitment to embracing new challenges and leveraging my skills to thrive in the dynamic field of web development.

In 2018, I joined Liberty Software, where I assisted in the overhaul and development of their corporate website. While there, I developed new assets and components that will ease another transition for the next forseeable future. Transitioning to Lowe's in 2020, I assumed greater responsibilities and progressed in my journey. With Lowe's, I led the development and creation of multiple projects, including an overhaul of their employee intranet space. I also worked with the marketing and sales team to develop a new social media ad campaign that garnered a 25% increase in engagement and clicks, which led to a 10% increase in targeted sales.

Joining Mary Kay in 2022, I continued my journey of delivering impactful results. I simplified the code that was originally used to create web assets, which lead to campaigns being developed quicker. I also collaborated with the digital team to create and develop new targeted websites for new product launches and deals, which led to a 25% boost in engagement across all channels.
